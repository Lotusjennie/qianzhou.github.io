Dr. Qian Zhou is a member of the Chinese Association for Cryptologic Research, a Senior Member of CCF, an Executive Committee Member of the CCF Technical Committee on Information Systems, a Senior Member of the Chinese Institute of Command and Control, and an Advisor to the Jiangsu Software Industry Talent Development Foundation. She serves as a reviewer for multiple SCI/EI journals including *IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems*, *IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics*, *Journal on Communications*, and *Journal of Software*, and has long provided information security consulting services to government agencies and enterprises.
